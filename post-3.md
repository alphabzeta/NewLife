# A Fun Day from Old Life
I remember Kabalak Sokak in Ortakoy Neighborhood of Istanbul where I lived through my childhood from 2 years old to 14. Kabalak is a plant and it always sounded funny to me with its interesting phonetics. It was a lovely place in the 70s and 80s. I was one of those children of X generation whose playground were streets.
