# A Fun Day from Old Life
I remember Kabalak Sokak in Ortakoy Neighborhood of Istanbul where I lived in my childhood from 2 years old to 14. Kabalak is a plant and it always sounded funny to me with its interesting phonetics. Ortakoy was a lovely place in the 70s and 80s. I was one of those children of generation X whose playground were streets and my streets were Ortakoy's.
