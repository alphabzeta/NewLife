# 14.04.2026
My life started to change when I stepped into the entrance of the Polyner Research Center in Istanbul in 1999 and it was always for the better. Something had to change in my life in the new millenium. It turned out that what had to change was belonging to one more family. The new family is no lesser than my biologcal family. It is an academic family but I now confuse it with my own family.

When I am with members of my family, I am always surrounded with beauty. Beauty in the eyes of the members, beauty in the pictures on the walls. Another perpetuity is the unyielding determination to contribute to science. 

Healing started right there, right then.
